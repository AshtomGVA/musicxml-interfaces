Interface Glissando

Glissando and slide elements both indicate rapidly moving from one pitch to the other so that individual notes are not discerned. The distinction is similar to that between NIFF's glissando and portamento elements. A glissando sounds the half notes in between the slide and defaults to a wavy line. A slide is continuous between two notes and defaults to a solid line. The optional text for a glissando or slide is printed alongside the line.
